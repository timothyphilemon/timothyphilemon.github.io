About
Free movie screenings happen often, but RSVP links are scattered and buried across the web. I built custom Python scraper agents to aggregate public listings in real time so users can receive email or text alerts with free passes before they sell out. Now serving thousands across 25+ U.S. cities — backed by LvlUp Ventures.
